The Glastimate Premium Tempered Glass Screen Protector employs revolutionary technology to protect you mobile's screen damage and scratches. 

GLASTIMATE premium tempered glass screen protector is made with specially processed glass which not only provides for surface protection but it has also been reinforced to increase shock absorbency.
It is not only effective in serving its purpose, it is also user friendly as the back of the glass is covered with a strong silicon adhesive for easy installation. Users can also also be rest assured that once installed, there will be no existing gaps between the mobile phone screen and the Glastimate premium tempered glass screen protector therefore it will not affect the performance and sensitivity of touch screen phones.
